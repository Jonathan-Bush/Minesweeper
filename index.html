Welcome to Jonno's Minesweeper!

To play, click a tile on the board. The numbers represent the number of mines in uncleared tiles
in the 8 surrounding squares. Mark tiles you believe to have a mine with a flag using right
click. Once you uncover all safe tiles, you win!

For difficulty, appearance, and control options,
click the settings button in the top right.
